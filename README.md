#  Sales Analytics Project (Excel + Power Pivot)

##  Project Purpose
The purpose of this Sales Analytics project is to empower businesses to monitor, evaluate, and improve their sales performance. By analyzing structured sales data, the project helps identify sales patterns, track key performance indicators (KPIs), and support data-driven business decisions.

---

##  Business Problem
Sales teams and management often struggle to extract meaningful insights from raw sales data. This project addresses the following business needs:

- Clear visibility into **net sales performance**
- Identification of **top-performing and underperforming products**
- **Market-level comparison** against defined sales targets
- **Year-over-Year (YoY) growth analysis**

Raw sales data alone cannot answer these questions without proper data modeling, transformation, and analytical logic.
